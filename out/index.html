The Elle Story

Named "Elle" meaning "her" in French, our restaurant pays homage to all that is maternal, womanly, and goddess-like. Created by restaurateurs Jay Leitson and Izzy Schachner, Elle embodies elegance and sophistication.

"From-scratch Northern Italian and French cuisine meets Bohemian chic atmosphere. Every dish is prepared with love using the freshest seasonal ingredients."

— Elle Restaurant & Lounge

Step into our eclectic space featuring lush fabrics, sparkly chandeliers, and handsome furniture. Experience award-winning cuisine, craft cocktails, and an atmosphere that transforms every meal into an unforgettable occasion.
